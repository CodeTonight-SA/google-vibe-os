Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 December 2025

Summary

Your data stays on your device. Googol Vibe is a local-first application that connects directly to Google's APIs using your own credentials. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your emails, calendar events, files, or tasks
  • Your Google account credentials
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • IP addresses or device identifiers

How It Works

Googol Vibe connects to Google Workspace APIs using OAuth 2.0 credentials that you create in your own Google Cloud project. This means:

  • You control the OAuth application
  • Tokens are stored locally in ~/.googol-vibe/
  • All API calls go directly from your device to Google
  • No data passes through our servers

Optional Telemetry

Googol Vibe includes optional, disabled-by-default telemetry for crash reporting. If you choose to enable it, we collect only:

  • Error stack traces (no personal data)
  • Application version
  • Operating system type

This data is processed by Sentry and used solely to fix bugs.

Third-Party Services

When you use Googol Vibe, you interact with:

  • Google APIs - Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Sentry (if telemetry enabled) - Subject to Sentry's Privacy Policy

Data Storage

All application data is stored locally:

  • ~/.googol-vibe/credentials.json - Your OAuth client configuration
  • ~/.googol-vibe/tokens/ - OAuth refresh tokens
  • ~/.googol-vibe/config.json - Application settings

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to delete. To remove all local data, simply delete the ~/.googol-vibe/ folder and uninstall the application.
