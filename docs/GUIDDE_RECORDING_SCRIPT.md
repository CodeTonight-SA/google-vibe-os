## Guidde Tips

- **Magic Mic**: Talk as you record - Guidde transcribes and cleans up your script
- **Pause between actions**: 1-2 second pauses help AI identify step boundaries
- **Click deliberately**: Centered clicks on buttons work best for annotations
- **Keep it under 5 min**: Optimal engagement for software tutorials
